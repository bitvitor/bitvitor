System Features

Current Financial System

  • Inflationary (Unlimited supply)
  • Geographically limited transfers
  • Fees, taxes, and account maintenance
  • Involvement of intermediaries (Banks, Government)
  • Custodial system with risk of confiscation
  • Centralized control by the Central Bank
  • Transactions can be censored

Decentralized System

  • Fixed supply of 21 million units
  • Deflationary and infinitely divisible
  • Instant global transfers
  • Very low fees (approx. 0.005 EUR)
  • No intermediaries, peer-to-peer
  • Non-custodial and impossible to confiscate
  • High level of privacy and uncensorable

Installation Instructions:

  1. Download the app from Google Play or App Store.
  2. Go to Settings and select your language and currency.
  3. Perform a manual backup (write down the 12 words in a safe place).
  4. Done! You can now receive and pay with BCH.

Backup & Security

The wallets mentioned here are non-custodial. This means only you have access to your assets.

Your recovery phrase of 12 or 24 words is the key to your money. If you lose these words, you lose access to the funds. Never share them with anyone and do not store them in digital format.
